Frequently Asked Questions

PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares) is a payout system that rewards miners based on their contribution to the last N shares before a block is found. This system discourages pool hopping and rewards loyal miners who contribute consistently. When a block is found, the reward is distributed proportionally among miners who submitted shares during the PPLNS window.
Payments are processed automatically when your balance reaches the minimum threshold of 0.005 XMR. Payments are typically sent within a few hours after meeting the threshold. You can check your pending balance anytime by entering your wallet address in the Miner Statistics section.
Use our XMRig Config Generator above! Simply enter your wallet address and worker name, then copy the generated configuration. Save it as config.json in your XMRig folder. You can also use the command line: xmrig -o euroxmr.eu:4242 -u YOUR_WALLET -p x
RandomX is CPU-optimized. Typical hashrates: Intel i5 = 3-5 KH/s, Intel i7/i9 = 5-10 KH/s, AMD Ryzen 5 = 5-8 KH/s, AMD Ryzen 7/9 = 8-15 KH/s. Server CPUs like AMD EPYC can reach 20+ KH/s. Enable huge pages for 20-30% performance boost!
Yes! SSL/TLS encrypts your connection and prevents ISP throttling or man-in-the-middle attacks. Use port 4343 with stratum+ssl:// for encrypted mining. This is especially important on public networks or in countries with restrictive internet policies.
